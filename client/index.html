Who can use Levisys Flywheel Technology?

Levisys Flywheel Systems help produce, store, and manage the creation of renewable energy is a more efficient, cost-effective manner.
Versatile and revolutionary, FW applications can be used to run and maintain smart grids and data centres, in railways management, to power fast chargers for electric vehicles and by utility companies who wish to meet existing energy transition challenges.

How does a Flywheel work ?

A flywheel allows for the conversion and storage of electrical energy as kinetic energy, allowing users to dynamically regulate their energy needs, in the most efficient way possible, with least wastage.
Our rotor-based system will help you transform your energy usage lifecycle, allowing you to store excess energy from a grid, reconvert this kinetic energy back into electrical energy to supply it to the grid, in the most optimal manner.
Flywheels are very well suited for applications that require high efficiency, fast response times, high cyclability and minimal energy discharge. Our technology is designed to withstand extreme temperature conditions, and is built to last!
